Severity

1 - A problem that critically impacts AMC’s ability to do business, either circuit-wide or at a specific theatre.  Examples include:

POS Server Down

Issues involving majority of circuit

Unable to perform sales on either concession or box transactions

50%+ of Point of Sale Stations and/or Kiosks down

50%+ of ticket printers down

Unable to perform close day

Unable to perform open day

Unable to process loyalty transaction

Unable to authorize credit cards

Unable to settle credit batches

Issues with KPS software/hardware impacting 20% or more of all transactions

2 - A problem that significantly impacts AMC’s ability to do business, either circuit-wide or at a specific theatre.  Examples include:

Issues with sales files

Issues with import of files

Issues with EOD transmit files

Wrong business date

0-50% of Point of Sale Stations and/or Kiosks down

0-50% of ticket printers down

Slow system response

3 - A problem that has limited impact on AMC’s ability to do business.
